1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or physical address.

  • We do not use cookies or web tracking technologies.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Photo processing: Your uploaded photo is sent to our AI service (Google Gemini or OpenAI) to generate a hairstyle preview. The photo is used solely for this purpose.
  • Service delivery: To process your hairstyle requests, manage your credits, and deliver results.
  • Improvement: Aggregated, anonymized usage data helps us improve the App.

3. Photo Storage and Deletion

  • Uploaded photos and generated results are stored in Firebase Storage associated with your anonymous user ID.
  • Photos are used exclusively for hairstyle generation — never for training AI models, advertising, or sharing with third parties.
  • You can delete your generation history within the App.
  • If you uninstall the App, your anonymous account and associated data will be automatically deleted after 30 days of inactivity.

5. Data Security

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption.
  • Firebase Storage and Firestore are secured with authentication rules — users can only access their own data.
  • Server-side operations use Firebase Admin SDK with restricted permissions.
  • No payment card data ever touches our servers — all payments are handled by Apple/Google.

6. Children's Privacy

HairTry is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Data portability: Request your data in a portable format.
  • Objection: Object to certain processing of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hairtryapp@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data Retention

  • Active account data is retained as long as you use the App.
  • Inactive accounts and associated data are deleted after 30 days of inactivity.
  • Subscription transaction records are retained as required by law and Apple/Google Store policies.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in data centers located in the European Union (Firebase region: europe-west3, Frankfurt, Germany) and the United States (AI processing). By using the App, you consent to this transfer. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.
