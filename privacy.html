3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or physical address.
  • We do not collect location data (GPS, IP-based geolocation).

  • We do not use your photos for AI model training.

4. How Your Photos Are Processed

Transparency about photo processing is important to us:

  • Upload: When you select a hairstyle, your photo is uploaded to our secure server (Firebase Storage, encrypted in transit via HTTPS/TLS).
  • AI Processing: Your photo is sent to a third-party AI service (Google Gemini or OpenAI GPT Image) along with the hairstyle description to generate a preview. The photo is used solely for this single generation request.
  • Storage: Your uploaded photos and generated results are stored in Firebase Storage, associated with your anonymous user ID.
  • No Training: Under these providers' paid API data-processing terms, your photos are not used to train their AI models, and we never use them for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than delivering your requested hairstyle preview.
  • Limited Sharing: Your photos are never sold, used for advertising, or made publicly accessible. They are shared only with the AI providers named above (Google Gemini or OpenAI) — and only to generate the hairstyle result you requested.

7. Data Security

We implement technical and administrative measures to protect your personal data:

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption.
  • Firebase Storage and Firestore are secured with server-side authentication rules — users can only access their own data.
  • Server-side operations use Firebase Admin SDK with restricted permissions (principle of least privilege).
  • No payment card data ever touches our servers — all payments are processed by Apple App Store and Google Play Store.
  • Access to production systems is restricted and monitored.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Active accounts: Data is retained as long as you actively use the App.
  • Inactive accounts: Accounts with no activity for 90 days may be flagged for deletion. Data associated with inactive accounts is deleted within 30 days of the flagging.
  • Account deletion: You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at sberk.bilgic@gmail.com. We will process your request within 30 days.
